Irish Boxty




Description

Irish potato pancakes. Recipe from AllRecipes.


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups grated raw potatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup leftover mashed potatoes
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon skim milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Toss grated potatoes with flour in a large bowl. Stir in mashed potatoes until combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together egg and milk; mix into the potatoes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Drop in the potato mixture, forming patties about 2
    inches in diameter. Fry on both sides until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Drain on a paper
    towel-lined plate. Serve warm.
